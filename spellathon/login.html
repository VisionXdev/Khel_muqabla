Instructions

  • SPELLATHON is 45 minutes & consists of 3 levels.
  • LEVEL 01: SPELL IT RIGHT
    Unscramble 10 words and spell them correctly.
  • LEVEL 02: GRAMMAR FIX
    Fix grammar mistakes in 10 sentences.
  • LEVEL 03: MIX N MATCH
    Reconstruct 10 scrambled sentences with correct grammar.
